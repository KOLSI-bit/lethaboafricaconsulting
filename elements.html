03 Accordion / FAQ

How do I get started with Lethabo Africa Consulting?
Simply contact us through our website or phone and one of our consultants will discuss the best way to support your business.
Do you assist startups?
Yes. We support startups and established businesses with registration, compliance, and business support services.
Do you help with compliance documentation?
Yes. We help clients manage the documentation and processes needed to remain compliant and in good standing.
How are fees structured?
We operate on a transparent fee-only model — no commissions, no conflicts of interest.
Do you offer international services?
Yes, we serve clients in 30+ countries. Our digital-first approach means we can work with anyone, anywhere.
Can I switch advisors if needed?
Absolutely. Client satisfaction is our priority. If you'd prefer to work with a different advisor on our team, we make that transition seamless.
